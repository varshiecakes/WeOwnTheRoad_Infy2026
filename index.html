Road Journey

"Outsmart the hazards. Own the Road"

Our Pitch

Road Journey helps educate people about driving hazards to protect themselves and pedestrians, preventing accidents before anyone gets seriously hurt.

Theme Implementation

Our game promotes safety and innovation, using our app to ensure prevention of harm. Using immersive minigames relating to driving ensures our players understand what they need.

Why We Built This

Researching many different topics such as poverty, world hunger and wildlife conservation; Road safety stuck out to our team, especially after finding out 120 people die EVERYDAY from road hazards.
